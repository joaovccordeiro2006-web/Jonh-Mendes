About

Jonh Mendes

Jonh Mendes is a professional in the field of product design, specialized in creating products for luxury brands aiming to maximize the quality and reputation of their pieces He has been working in the industry for 15 years and has worked at two large studios in Lisbon He is currently freelancing by choice, seeking to gain more recognition for his work as an independent designer internationally within his specialized niche. He is always looking for new projects with increasingly prestigious reputations to further his professional growth, and is characterized as highly ambitious, perfectionist, and dedicated

In 2011, Jonh left the previous company that has been working for 2 years and move from USA to sign an contract with Riot Creative Agency, an product design company from USA, where he has been working for more than 2 years

In 2018, Jonh leave Ammunition Group for personal reasons, and after 3 months he decide starting freelancing for building more his own personal brand

In 2022, Jonh was also making one of the best luxury pieces in the world, colaborating with the famous company, Vacheron Constantin, making one of the best watches of the brand history

In 2009, next to finish his graduation, Jonh sign contract for "Adico", an business that works on the product design area

In 2013, Jonh move to the state of São Francisco, with the intuite of sign contract with one of the biggest product and brading design companies in the world, the Ammunition Design Group, an big company where Jonh has been working for 5 years and do his main biggest works in the luxury product design genre

The Freelancing Season

In 2020 Jonh made one of his biggest colaborations with the Cartier company main product design team, making one of the best product design pieces on the Cartier history brand
